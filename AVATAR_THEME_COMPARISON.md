## Advanced Combinations

### Contrast Play
- **High Contrast**: Noir Shadow + Light hair + Dark outfit
- **Low Contrast**: Pastel Air + Similar tone hair + Matching outfit
- **Medium Contrast**: Urban Dawn + Complementary colors

### Monochromatic
- Choose theme
- Select all elements in same color family
- Vary only lightness/darkness
- Result: Sophisticated, cohesive

### Complementary
- Choose warm theme (Velvet Dusk)
- Add cool accent (Steel blue hair)
- Balance with neutral outfit
- Result: Dynamic, eye-catching

### Analogous
- Choose theme
- Select adjacent colors from palette
- Smooth transitions
- Result: Harmonious, pleasing

**Pro Tip**: Start with a theme that matches your current mood, then customize from there. The theme sets the atmosphere, your choices add personality!
